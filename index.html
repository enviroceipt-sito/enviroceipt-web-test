The Problem

For Retailers:

Thermal paper & physical loyalty cards cost retailers thousands each month.

Thermal paper receipts are non recyclable and contain BPA which is dangerous to their staff and consumers.

Digital e-receipt barrier to entry is high - it takes up to 4 minutes at the point of sale to collect customer details

It can be difficult to engage customers with loyalty and offers at point of sale.

Paper receipts house shopper feedback information however the barrier to entry is too high for many customers.

Our Vision

An eco-friendly consumer-focused tech company that assists retailers in achieving goals around loyalty, offers and feedback, whilst also saving them significant time and money.

We believe that receipts, offers and loyalty are key for retailers and consumers alike and that a single dedicated App will be integral to the digital evolution in this space.

We will work with the now, to evolve to the future with all our retail partners.

Create consumer focused, but retailer engaged, technology that enables for a fast and efficient scan/click-and-go receipt collection at the Point of Sale and online.

Ultimately ridding the world of clutter causing, environmentally unfriendly paper receipts, whilst enabling retailers and customers to benefit from smart receipts.
