What we offer

We’re developing technology that can easily be integrated into existing retailer systems. Our product will enable consumer to scan or tap & go, taking their digital receipt within seconds.

For shoppers

We can help you easily track and manage their digital receipts. Ease of use, simplicity, and peace of mind are the three pillars employed for the design of client-facing Enviroceipt applications.

For retailers

We can help streamline your receipt claim process. We envisage helping vendors to provide offers, loyalty & promotions to relevant customers with ease.
